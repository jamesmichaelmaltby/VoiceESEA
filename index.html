Our Mission

We want to eliminate racial discrimination against East and South East Asians (ESEA) by educating about, and ampliyfying voices of ESEA within the community. Our aim is for everyone to feel safe, respected and proud.


Example of a List Section

Our first aim is to educate and develop an inclusive society that is anti-racist. We will achieve this through the following activites.

  1. Educate on how to become anti-racist, which involves more effective, proactive work.
  2. Provide ESEAs and minority communities with educational resources to help overcome race-based challenges.
  3. Raise awareness about the ESEA diaspora experience in society.
